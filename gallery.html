What is sustainability?

Sustainability is to progressively make our lives and our planet better, enviromentally, socially and economically.

How does it impact us?

It effects us and our earth along with the rest of the life in the planet. bad enviromental, social and economic sustainability can and would deteriorate our daily lives and our lives.

How do we aim to take action?

As mentioned previously, the United Nations came up with a plan to prosper life as we know it. There are seventeen goals in total which are interlinked to each other.




Sea Life!

The seas and oceans are being polluted daily. From spilled oil to regular every day use plastics to microplastics that the human eye cannot see. Each day, the animals in our waters are dying from our faults and it is our goal for 2030 to preserve life in our waters.

Wild Life!

Life on land is just as endangered as life below water. Animals being slaughtered daily for our foods and for sporting and entertainment, for our clothing even. For the 2030 goals, we as humans aim to preserve life on land also.

Education !

Everyone deserves education, and not just education from primary to end of secondary, but third-level education too. Good quality education is lacked in may countries around the world and the 2030 goals aim to see to this goal by 2030.
